Upload Guidelines

Please read these guidelines carefully before contributing a previous year paper.

Clear & Readable

Upload clear and readable images only. Avoid shaky or dimly lit photos that are hard to read.

Supported Formats

We support standard image formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP. No PDFs are allowed.

One Paper at a Time

Upload one question paper per submission. Do not mix multiple different exam papers.

Accurate Details

Select the correct Course, Semester, Subject, and Examination Year when filling out the form.

Complete Papers

Upload complete papers whenever possible. Ensure all pages of the question paper are included.

What NOT to Upload

Do not upload personal documents, notes, assignments, duplicate papers, or blurred images.

Quality Review

All uploads may be reviewed by moderators before being published to ensure community quality.

No Sensitive Info

Do not upload content containing personal or sensitive information. Protect your privacy.

File Size

Keep file sizes reasonable for faster uploads and better performance for everyone downloading.

Help the Community

By contributing high-quality previous year papers, you are directly helping fellow students prepare better for their examinations. Thank you for your support!
