#About

KU Previous Year Papers is a student-driven platform designed to help students prepare for examinations by providing easy access to previous years' question papers from Kashmir University.

The platform allows students to search papers by semester, subject, and subject code, making it quick and convenient to find relevant exam material.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make previous year papers accessible to every student while encouraging the academic community to contribute and support one another. By creating a centralized repository, we aim to reduce the time students spend searching for papers and help them focus on their preparation.

Key Features

  • Organized papers by Semester, and Subject.
  • Fast search and filtering.
  • Student-contributed paper uploads.
  • Secure upload moderation to maintain quality.
  • Mobile-friendly and responsive interface.
  • Clean, modern, and easy-to-use design.

Community Driven

This platform grows through student contributions. Every uploaded paper helps future students prepare better for their examinations. Together, we can build a comprehensive collection of previous year papers for everyone.

Disclaimer

This website is an independent student initiative and is not officially affiliated with Kashmir University or any other educational institution. All uploaded papers remain the responsibility of the contributors and are intended solely for educational and exam preparation purposes.
